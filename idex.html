Calcium thresholds

🚫Never give calcium gluconate via peripheral IV — tissue necrosis risk. Use UVC. Never rapid bolus — cardiac arrest risk.
Cranial USS — Resistance Index

RI = (PSV − EDV) ÷ PSV · Anterior cerebral artery. USS: within 24h · day 3–5 · post-rewarming. MRI: day 4–5.

RI 0.60–0.80NormalStandard monitoring
RI >0.90Severe oedemaEscalate · neurology · consider MRI
RI <0.55HyperaemiaSevere HIE — repeat USS in 12h

📖 Required documentation
  • Cooling eligibility checklist (signed by specialist)
  • Thompson Score at 0, 4, 8, 12, 24, 48, 72 hours
  • Modified Sarnat staging at initial assessment
  • aEEG pattern at 24, 48, 72 hours and each shift handover
  • Cooling observation chart — temp q15 min induction, then hourly
  • Fluid balance chart — 4-hourly running total, 24h cumulative
  • Seizure chart (commenced at initiation)
  • Rewarming chart — temp hourly until 36.5°C
  • Biochemical monitoring log (BSL, iCa, Mg, electrolytes)
  • Daily senior ward round documentation
